# Regulatory Outsourcing Classification Tool

An interactive decision tree for classifying outsourcing arrangements under DORA, MaRisk AT 9, EBA GL, and § 25b KWG.

## What it does

Answer 5–7 questions about a vendor arrangement and get an instant classification covering:

- Material vs non-material outsourcing
- ICT vs non-ICT, CIF vs non-CIF
- Intra-group vs external arrangements
- BaFin notification requirements — type and timing
- CTPP concentration risk flags
- A ready checklist of what to prepare before contracting

## Who it's for

- **Outsourcing and compliance professionals** — classify new arrangements and reassessments faster, with the regulatory output needed to act
- **Internal stakeholders** — understand classification implications before a project starts to set timelines, involve the right teams, and avoid surprises
- **Service providers** — understand how regulated institutions classify your services and where contract gaps may exist

## Regulatory frameworks

| Framework | Scope |
|---|---|
| DORA EU 2022/2554 — Art. 28, 30, 31 | ICT third-party risk, CIF classification, CTPP oversight |
| MaRisk AT 9 | Material outsourcing definition, governance requirements |
| § 25b KWG | Legal basis for outsourcing obligations, materiality triggers, BaFin notification |
| EBA/GL/2019/02 | Outsourcing guidelines, critical function definition, due diligence requirements |
| ITS 2024/2956 | Register of Information templates |

## Scope

The tool covers:
- ICT and non-ICT outsourcing
- External and intra-group arrangements
- New classifications and reassessments
- Cloud and non-cloud services

## How to use

Open `index.html` in any browser. No installation, no dependencies, no data is collected.
